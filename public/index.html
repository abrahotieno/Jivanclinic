FAQ

Common questions

What is rTMS and is it safe?

rTMS (repetitive transcranial magnetic stimulation) is an FDA-approved, non-invasive treatment that uses magnetic pulses to stimulate specific areas of the brain. It has been extensively studied and proven safe with minimal side effects—most commonly mild scalp discomfort that resolves quickly.

How do I know which program is right for me?

Every client begins with a comprehensive assessment including health history, diagnostic testing, and personal goals. Our team then creates a personalized protocol combining the most appropriate therapies from both divisions.

What makes Jivan different from other wellness clinics?

Jivan is the only clinic in East Africa offering an integrated approach combining precision wellness with advanced neurotechnology. Our luxury environment, discreet service, and evidence-based protocols create outcomes that standalone treatments cannot achieve.

Do you offer corporate or family packages?

Yes. We design custom programs for executive teams, organizations, and families. Corporate retainers include priority booking, on-site assessments, and dedicated account management. Contact us for a confidential consultation.
